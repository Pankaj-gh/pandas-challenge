Pandas HW-HeroesOfPymoli

Below are the five observations based on the analysis done on the Heroes of Pymoli


###1) 77.17 % of the total purchases were made by young adults falling in the age group of 15-29 years old

###2) The number of total purchases gradually increases with age groups, with peaking at 20-24 and then gradually keeps decreasing then after with its lowest in the >40 group

###3) There are a total of 84% of male players that contribute ~83% towards the total purchase value

###4) The average purchase price by gender (3.02 dollars) is lower than the overall average purchase price ( 3.05 dollars ) only in the male cateegory.

###5) The most popular item is also the most profitable item, but it is not the most expensive item
